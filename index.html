About

Jewel came about because of the need to expand the capacity of Changi Airport's Terminal 1 as well as its open-air carpark. The stunning result is a mixed-use development with gardens, attractions, retail, dining, a hotel and facilities to support airport operations.

Want to experience an airport like no other? Visit Jewel!
